about me

I am passionate about coding.

I have creative heart with a perfectionist’s attention to detail and execution. This has served me well in my work as a music educator, a marching band director and musical conductor but none so well as in my work as a coder. I get profound satisfaction out of creating and problem solving. In my previous artistic endeavors, the quality of my product was largely subjective. I genuinely enjoy the binary correctness or incorrectness of computer code. If it’s right, it’s right! And if it’s wrong, there is a reason. Hunting down those reasons and inventing solutions are the way I joyfully prefer to spend my time! I am an enthusiastic, easy going collaborator.
